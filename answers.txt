Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git allows us to keep track of the different versions of our files, as well as work collaboratively with others without having to worry about directly making changes to a central document.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: One field that data science could be applied to is medicine. Statistical testing could be used to gauge the effectiveness of a particular serum on a given disease compared to its competitors. Further these results could then be visualized to showcase this result to the general public. 

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the commit history, whereas rebasing redoes the commits from the external branch into the original with new commits.
